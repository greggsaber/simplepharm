Welcome

Welcome to the PharmaFlow Prescription Refill Portal.

This portal allows patients with existing repeat prescriptions to submit refill requests before arriving at the pharmacy.

Completing your request in advance allows our pharmacy team to prepare your medications earlier, helping to reduce waiting time during collection.

Before You Begin

  • ✔ Prepare your NRIC / FIN.
  • ✔ Prepare your Date of Birth.
  • ✔ Ensure you have an existing repeat prescription.
  • ✔ Select only the medications you require today.
